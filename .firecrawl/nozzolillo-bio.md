**Anthony A. Nozzolillo, Esq.** is seasoned attorney with over twenty (20) years experience representing individual and corporate buyers, sellers, and lenders in both routine and complex residential and commercial real estate closing transactions; commercial and residential leasing agreements (lessor and lessee); mortgage refinancing- representing national and local lenders in closings on both first and second residential and commercial loans.

He serves as Chief Legal Counsel to a Real Estate Title Insurance Company, **On Point Land Services Corporation**, and is General Counsel to **Keen Settlement Services, Inc**. a Loan Settlement Company, both located in Merrick, Long Island, New York, serving as trusted legal advisor and representative to the company’s ownership team. He also serves as **Of Counsel** to the _Litigation_ and _Real Estate_ Departments of **Turturro Law, P.C**., a full-service Law Firm in Staten Island, New York.

He is well versed in all aspects of Landlord/Tenant matters (Evictions/Holdover Proceedings/Non-Pay Proceedings/Breach of Warranty of Habitability), as well as Title and Title Insurance, and possesses vast knowledge of Real Estate Litigation and dispute resolutions (Foreclosures, Breach of Contract Disputes, Escrow Deposit Disputes, Title Disputes (Quiet Title/Notice of Pendency), Boundary Line Disputes, Adverse Possession).

Mr. Nozzolillo is a frequent guest speaker, lecturer, and continuing education instructor at Realtor and Mortgage Broker conventions involving conventional and non-conventional closing transactions, real estate litigation, first time homebuyers, mortgage financing, and all facets of Title Insurance.

Although the current mainstay of his practice concentration is Real Estate Law, having run his own Private Law Firm for almost a decade, Mr. Nozzolillo provides additional legal services not limited to: _Drafting of **Wills and Trusts/Estate Planning**; **Business Law;** (Corporate Entity Formation, Asset Purchases, Business Contract/Agreement Drafting); **Civil Litigation Representation** (Personal Injury, Slip and Falls, Torts, Arbitration, Mediation); **Small Claims Court Matters**; and **Contract Law** (Partnership Agreements, Independent Contractor Agreements, Prenuptial Agreements, etc.)._

Additionally, Mr. Nozzolillo serves as Counsel to multiple Condominium and Cooperative Buildings, Management Companies and Restaurants situated in Nassau and Suffolk County, Long Island, New York. He represents celebrity clients, business owners, as well as High Net Worth Investors.

He is the honoree recipient of awards from: The Long Island Herald Newspaper; Long Island People’s Choice Awards; Long Island Business News (LIBN); The New York Association of Mortgage Brokers (NYAMB); Martindale-Hubbell, and The American Registry. His written legal articles have been published and circulated in local newspapers, magazines, as well as social media and network platforms.

Mr. Nozzolillo received a **_Bachelor of Science (B.S.)_** in **_Accounting_** from the State University of New York at Albany (School of Business) and his **_Juris Doctorate (J.D.)_** from Hofstra University School of Law. He is an active _Member_ of the: New York State Bar Association (Sustaining Member); Nassau County Bar Association; American Bar Association; American Association of Notaries; and _Affiliate Member_ of: Long Island Board of Realtors (LIBOR) and New York Association of Mortgage Brokers (NYAMB). In addition, he is a licensed New York State Real Estate Broker.
